Frequently Asked Questions

What types of tests are included?

Each box includes tests for at least three different health markers. All tests are approved for use in Canada. The exact number of tests may vary depending on the biomarkers selected.


Who picks the tests?

We default to our recommendations, ensuring you receive the most relevant tests for the season. However, you also have the option to swap and select your own tests based on your preferences.


How does testing work?

No lab or appointment needed. Each test is self-administered at home and works like a pregnancy or COVID-19 rapid test. You’ll get results within 15 minutes.


What does early access mean?

We’ll notify you when ABUNDL becomes available for purchase. Early access members get first dibs and may receive a discount on their first boxes.


How much does it cost?

ABUNDL is $49/month. We aim to include between $100–$200 of retail value in each box. Early access members may receive a discount on their first boxes.


Can I cancel any time?

ABUNDL is billed quarterly, but you may pause, skip, or cancel at any time—no questions asked.
