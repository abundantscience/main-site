Every minute matters.

Heart disease is the leading cause of death in Canada, and when cardiac events occur, time is critical. But what happens when your local health facility doesn't have cardiac testing capabilities? Many Canadians, especially in rural and remote areas, face this reality - where suspected heart attacks mean expensive emergency transfers or going without proper testing.

Health Canada has approved a revolutionary 3-in-1 cardiac rapid test that detects Troponin I, CK-MB, and myoglobin in just 15 minutes. It's the only Health Canada-approved point-of-care cardiac test available to clinicians across the country. This technology can bring cardiac testing to any health facility - from remote nursing stations to workplace clinics - enabling proper cardiac assessment where it simply wasn't available before.
